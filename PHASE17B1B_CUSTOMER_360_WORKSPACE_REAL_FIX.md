# Phase 17B.1B – Customer 360 Workspace Real Fix

This hotfix makes the Customer 360 Workspace visible and usable from the Customers list.

## Added / Fixed
- Customer name opens Customer 360 Workspace.
- Action button changed to **Open Workspace**.
- Removed confusing row-level Report button from primary customer list actions.
- Customer 360 page now clearly shows:
  - Customer header and contact details
  - Summary cards
  - Quotations tab
  - Projects tab
  - Inquiries tab
  - WhatsApp tab
  - Service tab
  - Documents tab
  - Timeline tab
  - Quick Actions
- New customer and edited customer redirect to Customer 360 Workspace after save.

## Notes
- Invoice and payment tabs are intentionally not added because those modules are not fully ready yet.
- This phase does not change database structure.
